Privacy Policy for Kids Math Game

Effective Date: February 01, 2025

Thank you for using the "Kids Math Game." Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data and ensure compliance with applicable laws, including the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation for Kids (GDPR-K).

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data from users of our app. No names, email addresses, location data, or other personally identifiable information is gathered.

2. Third-Party Services We Use

To improve the app experience, we use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Crashlytics & Notifications): Helps us analyze crashes and send important updates via push notifications.
  • Google AdMob: Displays ads within the app. We ensure all ads comply with child-safety guidelines, and "Child-Directed Treatment" is enabled to meet COPPA and GDPR-K standards.

3. Advertisements

We use Google AdMob to monetize the app. AdMob may collect non-personalized data to serve relevant child-safe ads. These ads comply with Google’s Families Policy.

4. Permissions Used

Our app requests notification permission to send push notifications via Firebase. No other device permissions (camera, storage, microphone, etc.) are required.

5. Compliance with Child Protection Laws

Since our app is designed for children, we strictly follow COPPA and GDPR-K guidelines:

  • No personal data is collected.
  • No user accounts or sign-ups are required.
  • Only child-safe ads are displayed.
  • Parental consent is not required, as we do not collect any child data.

6. Data Security

We do not store or share any personal data. However, third-party services like Firebase and AdMob may process limited, non-personalized data as per their policies.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected within the app and on our website (if applicable).
